# Default Wedding Planning Blueprint

Seed content for a new wedding planning board. Each `##` heading is an Epic.
Each bullet under it is a Task to create and link to that Epic.

This blueprint assumes a multi-event Indian wedding. Drop or rename sections
that don't apply (e.g. Sangeet, Rituals & Ceremony Logistics) and add
sections for traditions this blueprint doesn't cover.

## Guest Management
- Draft the full guest list and confirm final headcount per event
- Separate out a reception-only guest list for anyone not invited to the smaller/family functions

## Accommodation & Hospitality
- Plan accommodation and transport logistics for out-of-town guests

## Food & Catering
- Shortlist and confirm a caterer for every event and finalize the menu for each

## Vendors
- Confirm the decoration vendor and finalize the decor theme for each event
- Confirm photographer and videographer bookings, including deliverables and timelines
- Hold a final vendor briefing and assign a day-of coordinator for each event

## Invitations & Communication
- Design, order, and finalize the wedding invitation (physical card and/or digital invite)

## Clothing & Styling
- Finalize the bride's outfits and trousseau for every event
- Finalize the groom's outfits for every event
- Book the groom's grooming appointments ahead of the main events

## Gifts & Hampers
- Plan gift hampers and finalize the main gift exchanged between families
- Plan and order return gifts / favours for each event
- Pack ceremonial gift envelopes and small gifts exchanged between families

## Sangeet & Entertainment
- Plan and finalize performances and the running order for the entertainment night
- Shortlist, cut, and edit the music/audio for each performance
- Record and edit practice/performance videos ahead of the event
- Confirm DJ, sound, and lighting setup — event coverage, playlists, and processional

## Rituals & Ceremony Logistics
- Confirm the officiant and finalize the list of ritual items needed for the ceremony
- Plan the seating arrangement for the ceremony area
- Plan and confirm logistics for any processional — route, timing, transport

## Finance & Payments
- Create and maintain a master wedding budget tracker across all events and vendors
- Create a vendor payment schedule and track every advance and balance paid

## Wedding Week Operations
- Create event-wise run sheets and day-of schedules for every function
- Handle post-wedding tasks: honeymoon booking and thank-you follow-ups
